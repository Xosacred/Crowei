Our Mission

To project and advance the woman course through developmental stride in agriculture, business and career opportunities.





Give Support

We attend social functions to support our mother and initiator of CROWEI, Her Excellency, Dr. (Mrs.) Linda Ayade. We also give support to Governor, Senator (Prof.) Ben Ayade and show support to our members in the event of celebrating in their joys and condoling in their grief.

Women Empowerment

We ensure that women register and benefit from the Government Empowerment Entrepreneurship Programme (GEEP).

Social Participation

We participate in the Federal Government Initiative of MSME Exhibition and Clinics in Calabar and Abuja. We also take part in the Calabar Carnival as a non participating band.
